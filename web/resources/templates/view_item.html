Market Pricing

Evepraisal looks at a recent (around 5 minute delayed) market orders to estimate prices for items. For sell orders the minimum value is used because that is the lowest price that you can get if you purchase items from a sell order. For buy orders the maximum value is used because that is the highest price that you can get when selling items to a buy order.
